25 Highest-Grossing Movies of All Time



You could see the words "highest-grossing movies of all time" and think of movies like "Avatar" and "Avengers: Endgame" immediately. While those are actually two all-time box office champions, when you adjust for inflation, they don't top the list. Don't forget that film tickets cost only a fraction of what they cost now. There were billions fewer people on the planet as well, making the box office haul all the more remarkable for those retro classics. At least some of those classics, on the other hand, did not have current television or the rental market to deal with. What are the highest-grossing films of all time, Nit-picking aside? Let's explore it.
